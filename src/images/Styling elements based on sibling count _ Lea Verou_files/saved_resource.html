                                                          Per Lundgren 4 months ago

                                                          Experimented with this and wrote it as a Stylus mixin. Added some transitions for the demo interactions aswell. http://codepen.io/perlundgren/...

                                                          even-blocks(max)
  li
    display inline-block
  for li in 1
    li:nth-child(1):nth-last-child({li})
      width: (100% / li)
  for li in 2
    li:nth-child(1):nth-last-child({li})
    li:nth-child({li}):nth-last-child(1)
      width: (100% / li)
  for li in (3..max)
    li:nth-child(1):nth-last-child({li})
    li:nth-child(1):nth-last-child({li}) ~li
      width: (100% / li)

                                                          And then just use the function within your ul. Specify the max amount of items as the variable.

                                                          ul.menu
   li
       //li styling here
   even-blocks(12)
